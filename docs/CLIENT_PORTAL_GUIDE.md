# Client Portal Guide

The client portal is the external-facing workflow for sharing SEO progress with
client stakeholders without exposing internal agency operations.

## Client Access Scope

- Published reports through secure share tokens.
- Domain health summaries.
- Priority issues and resolved issue history.
- Recommendations approved for client visibility.
- White-label report domains when configured.

## Internal-Only Scope

- Billing settings.
- Workspace invitations and production roles.
- Integration secrets.
- Raw crawl snapshots.
- Internal issue notes.
- Unpublished report drafts.

## Client Workflow

1. Agency publishes a report.
2. Client receives a share link or accesses the white-label report domain.
3. Client reviews health score, issue summary, completed work, and next actions.
4. Agency keeps internal notes and technical remediation details in the main
   dashboard.

## Portal Readiness Checklist

- Report is published.
- Share token is active.
- White-label domain is verified, if used.
- Client-sensitive notes are not included.
- Branding and contact details are current.
